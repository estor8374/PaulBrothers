About Paul Brothers - Traditional Pottery Masters

Traditional Pottery Since 1995

Paul Brothers has been crafting exquisite mitti ke bartan and earthenware products in Ultam Pal for over 25 years. Our journey began with a simple passion for traditional pottery and has grown into a respected business known for quality craftsmanship.

Using locally sourced clay and time-honored techniques, we create functional and decorative pieces that bring natural beauty into your home. Each clay pot and matka is handcrafted with care, ensuring unique character and durability.

Our mitti ke bartan are made without any chemicals or artificial glazes, making them perfect for healthy cooking and water storage. The porous nature of clay allows for even heat distribution and natural cooling of water.
